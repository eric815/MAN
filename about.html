    ABOUT US

    We are your uniform solutions experts.

    With our roots established in manufacturing of uniforms since 2018 we have grown to be distinguished leaders within this category.

    Over the years, we have expanded manufacturing of uniforms to diverse industries and sectors. Today, we manufacture school uniforms, hotel uniforms, safety clothing, clinical linen among others. We have the capacity to produce up to 5,600 uniform sets, 11,000 shirts and blouses and 500 corporate suits monthly depending on the style and requirements.
